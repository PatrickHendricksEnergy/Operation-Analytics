# Portfolio Executive Summary

## Cross-Case Highlights
- Procurement KPI analysis shows the largest commercial scale with total order value 49,304,822.86 across 777 orders.
- Company purchasing dataset totals 1,240,580.53 across 500 transactions, with TechMart Inc. and Electronics leading spend.
- Supply chain dataset records total revenue 577,604.82 and total costs 52,924.58, with top product type skincare and top supplier Supplier 1.

## Portfolio Recommendations
- Supplier concentration: compare top suppliers across cases and prioritize strategic negotiations where spend is concentrated.
- Service performance: align delivery lag and compliance KPIs with supplier scorecards and quarterly reviews.
- Logistics efficiency: target transportation modes and routes contributing the highest costs for optimization pilots.

## What To Monitor Next (Portfolio KPIs)
- Total spend / order value by supplier (monthly)
- Compliance rate and delivery lag trends
- Revenue vs. costs by product type and transportation mode

## Methods & Assumptions
- KPIs are computed directly from each case dataset with consistent cleaning and aggregation logic.
- Forecasting is only applied where date fields exist.

## Limitations & Next Steps
- Datasets vary in scope and time coverage; avoid direct unit-level comparisons across cases.
- Currency codes are provided as USD for monetary values; confirm actual currency if integrating external data.
- Expand with supplier hierarchy and contract metadata for richer benchmarking.
